About

MACE

MACE (Malabar Association of Computer Engineers) is a Computer Science & Engineering association formed by the students of Malabar College.The association is active from November 2016.

Guidance

MACE is managed by the Teaching / Non Teaching Staffs and Students of Computer Science department.The association provides Technical as well as Non Technical (Arts,Sports & Games) abilities to its members.
